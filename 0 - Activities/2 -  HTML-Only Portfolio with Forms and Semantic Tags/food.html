Favorite Food: Humba

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or shoulder (cut into chunks)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 pieces bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup salted black beans (tausi)
  • 1/2 cup banana blossoms (optional)
  • Hard-boiled eggs (optional)

Procedure

  1. Marinate pork in soy sauce, vinegar, and pineapple juice for 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion in a pot. Add the marinated pork (reserve the marinade) and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the marinade, add sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil.
  4. Lower heat, cover, and simmer for 45-60 minutes until pork is tender, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add tausi and banana blossoms (if using) in the last 10 minutes.
  6. Optional: Add hard-boiled eggs before serving.
  7. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
